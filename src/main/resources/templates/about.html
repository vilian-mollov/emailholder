About Email Holder

Email holder app is all about storing and managing personal emails. You can save email addresses. You can add definition/description for every single email that has been saved. You can add your list of sites registered by particular email.

List of sites

Every email has its own list. The list represents the sites that the current email is used for account or some sort of registration.

Description

You have the opportunity to insert description for every email that you have saved. This is additional information about the email that you can store and access any time.
