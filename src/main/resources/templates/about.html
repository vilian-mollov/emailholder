About Email Holder

Email holder app is all about storing and managing personal emails. You can save email addresses. You can add definition/description for every single email that has been saved. Also you can add your passwords and list of sites.

List of sites

Every email has its own list. The list represents the sites that the current email is used for account or some sort of registration.

Passwords

You can add password for every email. Don't misspell the password, because the app won't know if that is the right one. To ensuring precision you have to add the password two times. Try not copy and paste but type it manualy. This way the you have lower chance to make mistake.

Description

You have the opportunity to insert description for every email that you have saved. This is additional information about the email that you can stor and access any time.
